Privacy Policy - Qibla compass

Effective Date: January 19, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Qibla compass. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and handle your information when you use our application.

2. Information We Collect

Qibla compass collects the following information:

  • Location Information: We require access to your device's location (GPS coordinates) to calculate the accurate Qibla direction (direction towards the Kaaba in Mecca) from your current position.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your location information solely for:

  • Calculating the Qibla direction based on your geographic position
  • Providing accurate compass functionality for prayer direction

4. Data Storage & Retention

All location data is processed locally on your device. We do not:

  • Store your location data on our servers
  • Transmit your location data to any third parties
  • Share your location data with any external services
  • Collect any personally identifiable information (names, email addresses, etc.)

5. Third-Party Services

Qibla compass does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services. We do not integrate with any external platforms that could access your data.

6. Permissions Required

The app requires the following Android permissions:

  • ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION - For precise Qibla direction calculation
  • ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION - For approximate location when GPS is unavailable

These permissions are only used to determine your location for Qibla calculation and are not used for any other purpose.

7. Children's Privacy

Qibla compass is suitable for users of all ages. Since we do not collect personally identifiable information and all data remains on your device, our app is safe for users under 13 years of age.

8. Data Security

Since all data remains on your device and is not transmitted or stored externally, your location information remains secure within your device's local storage.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or suggestions about this privacy policy or our data practices, please contact us.

Contact Method: Please reach out through the contact options provided on the Google Play Store listing for Qibla compass.
